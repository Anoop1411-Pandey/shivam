Our Services

We offer a wide range of eye care services to meet the needs of our patients. Our services include:

Comprehensive Eye Exams

Detailed eye examinations using advanced diagnostic equipment to assess your eye health. Our comprehensive exams include visual acuity testing, refraction assessment, intraocular pressure measurement, and retinal examination to detect early signs of eye diseases.

Duration: 45-60 minutes

Includes: Vision screening, glaucoma testing, retinal photography

Cataract Surgery

Safe and effective cataract surgery using the latest phacoemulsification technology. We use premium intraocular lenses to restore clear vision and reduce dependence on glasses after surgery.

Technique: Micro-incision phacoemulsification

Recovery: 2-4 weeks

Success Rate: 98%+

Laser Eye Surgery

Advanced laser treatments for various eye conditions, including LASIK for refractive errors. Our state-of-the-art laser technology provides precise corneal reshaping for improved vision.

Types: LASIK, PRK, SMILE

Procedure Time: 15-30 minutes

Vision Improvement: Within 24-48 hours

Glaucoma Management

Comprehensive care for glaucoma, including medical management and surgical options. Early detection and treatment are crucial for preserving vision and preventing irreversible damage.

Treatments: Eye drops, laser therapy, surgery

Monitoring: Regular pressure checks

Prevention: Early detection programs

Pediatric Eye Care

Specialized eye care services for children, including routine exams and treatment of eye conditions. Our child-friendly approach ensures comfortable and thorough eye care for young patients.

Age Range: Infants to 18 years

Services: Vision screening, amblyopia treatment

Specialties: Strabismus correction

Emergency Eye Care

Prompt evaluation and treatment of eye emergencies, including trauma and sudden vision changes. Our emergency services are available to address urgent eye care needs immediately.

Available: 24/7 emergency consultation

Conditions: Eye injuries, sudden vision loss

Response Time: Immediate assessment
